Tic Tac Toe Game

Current Turn: X

Instructions


Objective: The goal is to be the first player to get three of your marks (X or O) in a row. The row can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

Taking turns: Take turns to place your mark (X or O) in an empty cell on the grid.

Placing a mark: On your turn, place your mark in one of the empty cells by clicking on it.


A player wins by being the first to get three of their marks in a row, which can be:


Horizontal: Three marks in a row on the same horizontal line.

Vertical: Three marks in a row on the same vertical line.

Diagonal: Three marks in a row on either diagonal line.
